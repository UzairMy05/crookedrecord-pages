Privacy Policy

The Crooked Record
Effective Date: July 5, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how The Crooked Record handles data when you authorize our TikTok integration through the CrookedRecord app on the TikTok Developer Platform. This policy applies solely to the automated content-publishing pipeline operating under the brand The Crooked Record.

Data We Collect

When you authorize our app via TikTok's OAuth 2.0 flow, we access only the following scopes:

  • user.info.basic — to verify the TikTok account identity linked to the authorization.
  • video.upload — to upload video files to TikTok's servers.
  • video.publish — to publish uploaded videos to the authorized TikTok account's profile.

We do not collect, store, or access any other TikTok account data, including but not limited to: direct messages, follower lists, analytics beyond what is returned in the publish response, or content from accounts other than the one that authorized the app.

How We Use Data

The data accessed via these scopes is used exclusively for automated video publishing. Videos produced by our content pipeline are uploaded and published to the authorized TikTok account according to a pre-configured schedule.

Data Storage & Retention

  • Access tokens are stored securely in encrypted environment files and are refreshed automatically via TikTok's OAuth refresh token flow.
  • Refresh tokens (365-day TTL) are stored using the same encrypted mechanism.
  • We do not retain copies of published videos beyond the duration of the upload-publish process, except as needed for operational logging (retained for 30 days, then deleted).
  • No user data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our publishing pipeline uses TikTok for Developers (for the Content Posting API) and Google YouTube API (for our parallel YouTube publishing pipeline). No third party receives your TikTok account data from us.

User Rights

You may revoke our app's authorization at any time through your TikTok account settings under "Authorized Apps." Upon revocation, we will cease all API calls using the associated credentials.
